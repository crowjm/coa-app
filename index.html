About Austin

Located in Central Texas on the eastern edge of the American Southwest, Austin is the capital of the state of Texas, the Lone Star State. The city dates back to the 1830s, when the first Anglo-American settlers arrived in the area, then part of Mexico. In 1837, settlers founded the village of Waterloo on the banks of the Colorado River, the first permanent settlement in the area. By 1839, Waterloo would adopt the name Austin and the frontier town would become the capital of the Republic of Texas. The City is named for Stephen F. Austin, the “Father of Texas.”

How Do I...?

Not sure how to pay your City of Austin utility bill or what can be recycled? We're here to help!

Pay My Bills?

The City of Austin offers a variety of payment options so you can pay your bill conveniently. We encourage you to pay online — it’s easy and it’s free. You can also pay by phone, by mail, or at an authorized pay station, or you can have your payment automatically drafted from your bank account.

Pay A Ticket?

The Austin Municipal Court accepts many different payment methods and offer many ways for your to make your payments. We accept cash (American dollars), money orders, cashiers’ checks, personal checks (from American banks in American dollars), and the following major credit cards: Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express.

You can pay online (24/7), in person at any court location, by mail, by phone, at a drop box, or via Western Union.

Adopt A Pet?

Austin Animal Center has wonderful pets looking for good homes. If you think you’re ready for the responsibility of adding a pet to your family, visit the Center or browse our adoptable pets online.

Austin Animal Center also has numerous programs and partnerships designed to help pets in the shelter, pets in the community and pets at home. Services range from free rabies vaccination clinics to low-cost / free veterinary care, including spay or neuter services.

Recycle?

Austin Resource Recovery provides curbside recycling collection to single-family households and some small scale multifamily customers in Austin. With our curbside recycling program, you can mix recyclable paper, plastic, metal and glass in your blue recycling cart - no sorting required!

Register to Vote in Texas?

You can register to vote via text by texting "Register" to 48683 (IVOTE). You will receive a postage-paid application by mail to sign and mail back.

You can also request an application from the Texas Secretary of State.
